Biography

Yiguo is currently an Assistant Professor in the PALM Lab at Southeast University. Before join SEU, Yiguo was a postdoctoral researcher in the CAMERA center and the Pering Laboratory at the University of Bath. She received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University in 2010 and 2018, respectively.

Yiguo’s research interests cover the fields of multimodal visual perception, computer vision and machine learning, specifically focusing on multimodal image processing and their applications in multimedia, smart healthcare, etc..

For more information, please refer to her PALM homepage.
