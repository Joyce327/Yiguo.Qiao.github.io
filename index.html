Yiguo Qiao is currently a Research Associate in CAMERA at University of Bath.

Biography

Yiguo's research interests cover the fields of image processing, computer vision, medical data analysis and motion capture, focusing specifically on depth map super-resolutionand and 3D stereoscopic vision.

Yiguo was previously working on 3D image processing and muti-view synthesis during her Ph.D. study at Xidian University. She then moved to University of Bath as Research Associate, working on thermal image processing and medical data analysis. Currently, she is engaged in research on motion retargeting in animation.

Yiguo received her Bachelor degree from Xidian University in 2010. She continued to pursue her Master and Ph.D. degree in the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding of the Ministry of Education of China, Xidian University, under the supervision of Prof. Licheng Jiao. After she got her Ph.D. degree in Artificial Intelligence in 2018, she is a Research Associate in University of Bath working with Prof. Darren Cosker.
